Why a graph database ?

  • Performance, here is a good summary by Emil Eifrem, founder of the Neo4j
  • Domain modeling: you use the language of node,properties and relationship to describe your domain (instead of e.g. UML) and there is no need to have a complicated O/R mapping tool to implement it in your database. You can say that Neo4j is “Whiteboard friendly” !
